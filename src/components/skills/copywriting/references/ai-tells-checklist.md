# AI Tells Checklist

## Common Signals

- Over-smooth paragraph rhythm with no sharp judgment.
- Template transitions: "in today's fast-paced world", "it is important to note".
- Generic summary endings after every section.
- Many balanced pairs but no concrete tradeoff.
- Abstract nouns stacked without a human action.
- Polite hedging where the brand should take a position.

## Diagnosis Output

```markdown
| Signal | Example | Why It Feels AI-Written | Rewrite Direction |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
```

## Rewrite Rules

- Add a specific audience, situation, number, or constraint.
- Vary sentence length intentionally.
- Replace summary with a decision, warning, or next action.
- Do not invent personal stories or fake customer evidence.
