# Social Proof

## Proof Types

| Type | Best For |
| --- | --- |
| Customer count | Adoption and reduced perceived risk. |
| Similar customer logo | Relevance to a target segment. |
| Quote | Emotional and practical credibility. |
| Case metric | Business outcome. |
| Community signal | Momentum and category validation. |

## Rules

- Match proof to the target segment.
- Prefer specific proof over large but vague numbers.
- Put proof before asking for high-commitment action.
- Do not use fake testimonials, unapproved logos, or cherry-picked metrics without context.

## Rewrite Example

```text
Weak: Many teams use us.
Better: 127 SaaS finance teams use it to close monthly reports without spreadsheet handoffs.
```
