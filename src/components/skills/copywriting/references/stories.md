# Stories

## Mini-Story Structure

```text
Before: what was hard?
Trigger: what changed?
Action: what did the customer do?
After: what improved?
Proof: how do we know?
```

## Use Cases

- Long-form landing pages.
- Case study snippets.
- Email intros.
- Objection handling.
- Founder or product narrative.

## Rules

- Use real or user-provided stories only.
- Keep the story tied to one claim.
- Do not add invented customers, dates, or quotes.
- Prefer a small true moment over a polished fake narrative.
