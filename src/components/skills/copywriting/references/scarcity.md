# Scarcity

## Valid Scarcity

- Limited event seats.
- Application-based cohort.
- Real inventory or implementation capacity.
- Time-bound pricing with a published end date.
- Regulatory, seasonal, or launch window constraints.

## Invalid Scarcity

- Fake countdown timers.
- "Only 3 spots left" for unlimited SaaS access.
- Permanent limited-time offers.
- Pressure without a reason.

## Pattern

```text
What is limited:
Why it is limited:
Deadline or quantity:
What happens after:
Low-pressure alternative:
```
