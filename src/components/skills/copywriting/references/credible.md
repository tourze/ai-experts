# Credible

## Proof Types

- external report;
- customer quote;
- before/after metric;
- live demo;
- transparent methodology;
- named constraint or limitation.

## Rules

- Put proof close to the claim.
- Give dates and sample sizes for metrics.
- Avoid impossible precision.
- Admit limits when they make the claim more believable.

## Pattern

```text
Claim:
Proof:
Source:
What this does and does not prove:
```
