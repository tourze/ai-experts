# Authority

## Valid Authority Signals

- Named customer case study with permission.
- Analyst report, certification, audit, or award.
- Expert quote with attribution.
- Public benchmark or reproducible test.
- Regulated compliance proof when relevant.

## Rules

- Put authority near the claim it supports.
- Do not borrow authority from unrelated logos or vanity metrics.
- Use dates for awards, reports, and certifications.
- Avoid "trusted by" unless the relationship is real and current.

## Pattern

```text
Claim:
Authority proof:
Date/source:
Why this proof matters to the buyer:
```
