# Concrete

## Replace Abstraction With

- numbers;
- named workflows;
- before/after states;
- screenshots or demo steps;
- customer roles;
- time, money, risk, or error metrics.

## Rewrite Example

```text
Abstract: Improve operational efficiency.
Concrete: Cut weekly invoice review from 4 hours to 25 minutes by flagging mismatched line items automatically.
```

## Checklist

- Can the reader picture the work?
- Can the claim be tested?
- Is the noun specific enough?
- Does the sentence name who benefits?
