# Competitive Alternatives

## Alternative Types

| Type | Examples | Why It Matters |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Direct competitor | Same category product. | Defines obvious sales comparison. |
| Adjacent product | Different category solving part of the job. | Reveals category confusion. |
| Internal process | Spreadsheet, manual workflow, scripts. | Often the real incumbent. |
| Do nothing | Status quo. | Explains urgency and budget barriers. |
| Services | Consultants, agencies, outsourced teams. | Changes value and pricing frame. |

## Discovery Questions

- What happens today when the problem appears?
- Who owns the workaround?
- What budget pays for the workaround?
- What fails when the workaround scales?
- Which alternative appears in lost deals?
