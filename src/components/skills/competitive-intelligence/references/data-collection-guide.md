# Data Collection Guide

## Evidence Sources

| Source | Use For | Reliability |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Product website and docs | Positioning, packaging, feature claims. | High for claims, not for usage. |
| Pricing page and terms | Packaging, limits, discount clues. | High when dated. |
| Changelog and release notes | Momentum and roadmap direction. | High for released work. |
| Job posts | Strategic investment and team buildout. | Medium; infer carefully. |
| Customer reviews | Pain, switching reasons, adoption barriers. | Medium; sample bias likely. |
| Sales calls and win/loss notes | Buyer objections and deal dynamics. | High internally, but anecdotal. |

## Record Format

```text
Evidence:
- Source:
- Date observed:
- Claim or fact:
- Screenshot/link:
- Confidence:
- Implication:
```

## Rules

- Capture dates for all market claims.
- Separate the competitor's own claims from customer-visible proof.
- Do not infer market strategy from one screenshot.
- Flag facts that require revalidation before sales or roadmap use.
